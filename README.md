{*]How to put your phone in Download mode

1.Turn off your phone and press vol up + vol down and plug the usb cable at the same time.

[*]How to flash

1.Go to ODIN and select recovery.tar from AP. (IMPORTANT: It's AP). Hit the flash button. 
auto reboot option doesn't matter at all but enabling is easier to reboot.

[*]How to boot into recovery (Read this is important)

Once you have hit the flash button, your phone will be rebooting to where SAMSUNG logo  and
custom binaries warning displaying. Then it will automatically rebooting to the same spot.

what you need to do is keep pressing VOL DOWN + BIXBY (POWER BUTTON) till it turn off (BLACK SCREEN)
and QUICKLY lift your fingers and start keep pressing VOL UP + BIXBY till it reboot to the SAMSUNG 
LOGO SCREEN and release only the BIXBY (POWER BUTTON). Not VOL UP button. You should keep pressing
VOL UP after you lift your finger from BIXBY for couple of seconds.

[*]How long time will it take to boot into custom recovery

It will be take 1 to 2 minutes to boot into custom recovery. you should be waiting and your device
will stay at SAMSUNG LOGO SCREEN till it boot to the custom recovery. that's it, have fun.

[*]Rolling back to stock recovery (Flashing stock recovery)

If you are in Linage OS Recovery, click to shutdown or poweroff button to turn off your device.
then press VOL UP + VOL DOWN (keep pressing) & connect your USB CABLE to put your device in
download mode. Go to ODIN flash tool and select recovery.tar file from your stock recovery
extracted folder into ODIN AP section (Important: It's AP). Hit the flash button and your
device will be normally rebooting into boot.

If you are in auto rebooting, you must remember this you always have a change to get into
download mode by pressing VOL UP + VOL DOWN & plugging USB CABLE when it came to black screen
before auto restarting attempt. also you can manually keep pressing VOL DOWN + BIXBY till it
comes to black screen and QUICKLY PRESS VOL UP + VOL DOWN & PLUG THE USB CABLE before the black
screen turn back to restart. (IMPORTANT: QUICKLY)
